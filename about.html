About

This Tutorial

Virtual Reality and Data Science are two modern fields of research that gained significant importance for the economy. One of the possible complementary benefits between these fields is the visualization of data.
This tutorial tries to provide an introduction into the visualization of data in Virtual Reality. Short overviews about Data Science and Virtual Reality followed by a specific example on how to realize data visualization in Virtual Reality are meant to be a starting point for students who are interested in new approaches for data visualization.
This website, the presented application and the tutorial are the result of an IAESTE internship at the Faculty of Electrical and Computing Engineering (FEEC) of University of Campinas with a duration of eight weeks in 2017. As the author of this content, I have tried to document my first encounters with Data Science, Virtual Reality and Unity in a way that helps other students to start into these research fields more quickly.

The Author
